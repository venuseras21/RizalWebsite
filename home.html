JOSE P. RIZAL

Father of Filipino Nationalism

Who is Jose Rizal?


Jose Rizal (June 19, 1861 - December 30, 1896) is one of the most revered figures in Philippine history. He was a multifaceted intellectual, a polymath, a Filipino nationalist and a political activist during the tail end of the Spanish colonial period of the Philippines. He is best known for his political writings that inspired the Philippine revolution and ultimately led to his execution by the Spanish colonizers. Due to his meritable actions he became considered as the national hero of the Philippines.
